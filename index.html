What is HackCT?

HackCT is the first ever hackathon powered by all of Connecticut’s regional makerspaces: MakerspaceCT, Spark Makerspace, Maker’s Guild, and MakeHaven, Danbury Hackerspace, NESIT, and CT Hackerspace. It's the place for programmers, engineers, and hobbyists in Connecticut to tackle new problems, try new skills, and find out about new technologies.
